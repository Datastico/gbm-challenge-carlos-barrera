What is the IPC?

The Consumer Price Index is a measure of the average change over time in the prices paid by urban consumers for a market basket of consumer goods and services.

Mexican Consumer Price Index (Local name: IPC - Índice de Precios al Consumidor) data at this website is provided by Grupo Bursátil Mexicano and can be found in this link.
